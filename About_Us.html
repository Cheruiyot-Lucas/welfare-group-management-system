  • About Kook Welfare Group
     Kook Welfare Group is a registered community-based welfare organization committed to promoting the financial and social well-being of its members.
    Officially registered in 2019 under the Ministry of Gender and Social Development, the group operates on principles of transparency, unity, and mutual support.
    Our goal is to provide a structured and reliable platform for managing group activities such as savings, contributions, loans, and welfare support.
    Through the use of digital systems, Kook Welfare Group ensures efficiency, accountability, and equitable access to services for all members.
  • Our Vision
     To be a trusted and leading welfare group that transforms lives through collective financial empowerment.
  • Our Mission
     To enhance community development and improve the welfare of our members by providing a secure, transparent, and user-friendly system that supports savings, investment, and mutual support.
  • Strategic Objectives
    • To promote a culture of saving and financial responsibility among members.
    • To provide an efficient and accessible platform for managing group finances.
    • To support members during times of need through structured welfare contributions.
    • To uphold transparency and accountability in all group operations.
    • To encourage investment and sustainable development initiatives within the group.
